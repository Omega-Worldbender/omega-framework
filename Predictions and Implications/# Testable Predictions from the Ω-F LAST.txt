## Prediction Philosophy

**Good predictions must be:**
1. **Specific:** Numerical values, not vague trends
2. **Falsifiable:** Clear criteria for being wrong
3. **Independent:** Not circular or self-referential
4. **Testable:** Achievable with current or near-future technology
5. **Surprising:** Non-obvious, couldn't be guessed without framework

**All 10 predictions meet these criteria.**

If even one prediction is definitively falsified, the framework
requires major revision or abandonment.

If multiple predictions are confirmed, framework gains credibility.
